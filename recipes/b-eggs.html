Boiled Eggs

Description

Eggs that have been boiled to a hardened edible state.

  • Egg(s)
  • Pot
  • Water

How to Make Boiled Eggs Step-By-Step

  1. Fill you pot up with water.
  2. Bring your pot to a boil using your stove-top.
  3. Once your water is boiled, gently place your eggs in the pot.
  4. Reference the picture above for desired egg texture.
  5. Enjoy.
