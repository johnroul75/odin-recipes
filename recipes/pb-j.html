Peanut Butter and Jam Sandwich

Description

2 slices of bread coated with Peanut Butter on one slice and Jam on the other slapped together.

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jam of your choice

How to make a PB&J Sandwich Step-By-Step

Here's a quick step-by-step of how to put your sandwich together:

  1. Take your 2 slices of bread and lay them out.
  2. Lather Peanut Butter on one slice of the bread.
  3. Lather jam on the other slice.
  4. Slap together.
  5. Enjoy.
